What We Do

We combine professional spedition services with flexible transport and logistics, giving you one partner from customs to final delivery.

A SHPED – Spedition Services

Professional support for customs and documentation so your cargo crosses borders smoothly and on time.

T1 Declarations

Preparation and handling of T1 transit documents for road transport across Europe.

Import & Export Documentation

IM4, IM5, IM7, IM9, EX1–EX3 and related documentation prepared correctly and on time.

Transit & Customs Support

Assistance with customs clearance, CMR, and transit procedures for your shipments.

Flexible road, air, and sea solutions tailored to your cargo, timing, and budget.

Road Transport

Air Transport

Fast solutions for time-critical shipments when every hour counts.

Sea Transport

Container and groupage options for cost-efficient long-distance flows.
